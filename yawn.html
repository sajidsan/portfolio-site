CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT

Yawn

While a frequently redesigned product, most alarm clocks have always left me feeling like they're solving for a symptom of a problem instead of the problem itself. Yawn was a chance for me to apply my understandings of behavioral economics and behavior change to a problem that is topical for us all on a nightly (and morning...ly?) basis.

I realized that the issue was that the person going to bed is not the same person that has to wake up the next morning

Starting with the foundation of BJ Fogg's behavior change model helped me frame the problem of sleep with an understanding of how a product could best move a user towards a desired behavior.
