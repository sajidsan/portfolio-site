Pique

While we have access to countless number of consumer goods, how can we find the products that we want without searching through the clutter? This question was one our team wanted to answer with our project, Pique.

The problem that Pique is helping to solve is that of the aspirational shopper.

People often have a certain look they're going for when they're shopping. But shopping is very distracting, and once we start it gets pretty haphazard.

Our approach is to create a recommendation engine that would help users keep track of their interests as they moved between mediums and across time.
