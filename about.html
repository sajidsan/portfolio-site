About me? I'm blushing.

I’m a product designer at Jut working on big data problems with an incredible team. I spend my after hours teaching a brilliant group of students at the Academy of Arts and trying to find people that’ll work on fun side projects with me. Before coming to the Bay area, I studied design research and interaction design at the IIT Institute of Design in Chicago and worked with the Studio for Design Research in Hong Kong. I've also taught a General Assembly Class on Research + Strategy. Check it out here. When not dorking out on design, you can find me noodling around in a sketchbook and playing bad hip hop covers on my Casio keyboard.

I just made a list of restaurants in SF with drawing paper on their tables.
Want to talk? Pick out a place and we'll sketch dorky diagrams, and talk shop :)
